Where am I?

This website supplements the Little Printer ecosystem by allowing you to easily setup some content sources to regularly send fun and interesting things to your Little Printer. Simply sign up and provide your device key URL and you're off to the races.

Do I need a Little Printer to use this?

Not at all. In fact, using these projects and following this guide will allow you to use a regular thermal printer (or even just your console!) with the littleprinter.nordprojects.co service. You can get reasonably priced compatible thermal printers such as the Peripage A6 or the Paperang P1 from China.
